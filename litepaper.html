Executive Summary

MADGER is a mascot-driven Solana memecoin project built around persistence, humor, and community. The project’s objective is to develop a recognizable character and media brand before launching a token.

Mission

Build a durable community known as The Burrow, publish consistent original content, communicate transparently, and expand the MADGER character into merchandise, animation, games, and other brand extensions when commercially justified.

Brand Principles

Persistence over panic. Humor over toxicity. Community over manufactured influence. Transparency over fake hype. Long-term brand development over short-term promises.

The Burrow

The Burrow is the MADGER community. Members are expected to welcome new participants, avoid dishonest promotion, challenge scams responsibly, and contribute through art, memes, education, moderation, or development.

Development Roadmap

Foundation: finalize brand, website, social channels, launch documentation, and content library.

Community: grow The Burrow organically through Daily Dig content, community activities, and transparent progress updates.

Launch: deploy a Solana token only after the launch structure, token allocation, liquidity approach, metadata, and security controls are documented and published.

Expansion: evaluate merchandise, collectibles, animation, games, wildlife partnerships, and licensing.

Token Information

Network: Solana. Proposed ticker: $MADGER. The token has not launched. No official contract address exists as of this version.

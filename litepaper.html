Executive Summary

MADGER is a mascot-driven Solana memecoin project built around persistence, sharp humor, and community. The project is developing a recognizable character and transparent launch structure before publishing a token contract.

The Mission

Build The Burrow into a durable community; publish consistent original content; communicate clearly; and grow MADGER beyond a token into a character brand with potential extensions in merchandise, animation, games, collectibles, and licensing.

Brand Principles

  • Persistence over panic.
  • Humor without toxicity.
  • Community over manufactured influence.
  • Transparency over fake hype.
  • Brand value before short-term promises.

The Burrow

The Burrow is the MADGER community. Members can contribute through art, memes, moderation, education, development, partnerships, and constructive participation. The official announcement channel is t.me/madgercoin; community discussion is at t.me/madgerburrow.

Proposed Token Framework

Network: Solana
Proposed ticker: $MADGER
Proposed supply: 1,000,000,000
Contract: Not yet deployed or announced

Final allocations, authority settings, liquidity structure, launch method, and verified contract address will be disclosed before launch. Until those details are published, no third-party contract should be treated as official.

Roadmap

Phase 1 — Build The Burrow: brand system, website, litepaper, official channels, original content, and launch documentation.

Phase 2 — Launch MADGER: deploy the token, publish the verified contract, establish liquidity, and release final launch disclosures.

Phase 3 — Grow The Gang: community campaigns, partnerships, listings, education, and recurring content.

Phase 4 — Take Over: evaluate merchandise, animation, games, collectibles, licensing, and other brand extensions.

Security

The official contract address will appear on madgercoin.com, @madgercoin, and the official Telegram announcement channel. Community members should verify addresses character by character and distrust unsolicited direct messages.

# MADGER FAQ

## What is MADGER?

MADGER is a community-focused token built on the Solana blockchain with a fixed supply and transparent project documentation.

## Why Solana?

Solana offers fast transaction confirmation times and low network fees, making it suitable for community token projects.

## What is the total supply?

1,000,000,000 MADGER.

## Can more tokens be created?

The project is designed around a fixed supply. The intention is to revoke the mint authority after launch verification so that no additional tokens can be minted.

## Are there transaction taxes?

No. The planned tokenomics do not include buy, sell, or transfer taxes.

## Where can I find official information?

Official project information is published through the repository documentation, the official website, and the project's official social channels.

## How can I support the project?

Follow official announcements, participate in the community, provide constructive feedback, and help others find accurate information.

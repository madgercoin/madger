# MADGER Authority Plan

## Mint Authority

Initial Holder:
- Creator Wallet

Action:
- Revoke permanently after token creation and verification.

Purpose:
- Prevent any future minting.

---

## Freeze Authority

Initial Holder:
- Creator Wallet

Action:
- Revoke permanently after launch verification.

Purpose:
- Ensure the token cannot be frozen after launch.

---

## Verification Checklist

- Token metadata verified
- Token supply verified
- Liquidity established
- Community announcement completed
- Mint authority revoked
- Freeze authority revoked

---

These actions are irreversible and must only be performed after all launch checks are complete.

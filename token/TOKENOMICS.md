# MADGER Tokenomics

## Supply

- Total supply: 1,000,000,000 MADGER
- Decimals: 6
- Additional minting after launch: Not permitted
- Transfer tax: 0%
- Buy tax: 0%
- Sell tax: 0%

## Allocation

| Category | Percentage | Tokens |
|---|---:|---:|
| Liquidity | 60% | 600,000,000 |
| Treasury | 20% | 200,000,000 |
| Community and Ecosystem | 10% | 100,000,000 |
| Operations | 7% | 70,000,000 |
| Creator Reserve | 3% | 30,000,000 |
| **Total** | **100%** | **1,000,000,000** |

## Allocation Purpose

### Liquidity

Used to establish and support public trading liquidity. The amount paired initially will depend on available SOL, launch valuation, slippage targets, and final liquidity strategy.

An allocation to the Liquidity wallet does not mean all 600,000,000 tokens must be deposited into the first liquidity pool.

### Treasury

Reserved for long-term project development, infrastructure, listings, legal and professional services, partnerships, and strategic initiatives.

### Community and Ecosystem

Reserved for community rewards, promotional campaigns, contributor incentives, partnerships, and future ecosystem programs.

### Operations

Reserved for marketing, software, hosting, design, moderation, development, and other operating expenses.

### Creator Reserve

Reserved for the founder. This allocation should not remain in the deployment wallet long-term. A transparent lock or vesting structure should be considered before public launch.

## Launch Principles

- Fixed maximum supply
- No hidden transfer taxes
- No post-launch minting
- No token-account freeze authority after verification
- Public disclosure of project wallet addresses
- Transparent disclosure of material treasury transactions
- No promises of guaranteed returns or price appreciation

## Finalization Status

This document is a planning specification.

Allocations remain subject to final review before any irreversible mainnet transaction. Wallet balances, liquidity funding, lock arrangements, vesting terms, and launch valuation must be confirmed separately.

# MADGER Wallet Registry

## Creator
Purpose:
- Token creation
- Authority management
- Authority revocation

## Treasury
Purpose:
- Long-term project reserves
- Ecosystem funding

## Operations
Purpose:
- Development
- Marketing
- Operational expenses

## Liquidity
Purpose:
- Initial DEX liquidity
- Liquidity management

## Community
Purpose:
- Airdrops
- Community rewards
- Promotions

## Security Rules

- Never store seed phrases in this repository.
- Never commit private keys.
- Verify every recipient address before sending funds.
- Keep hardware or wallet backups offline.
